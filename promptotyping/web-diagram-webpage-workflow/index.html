Proto-AGI Development Timeline (2020-2026)

The emergence of AGI through interconnected specialized systems

This diagram illustrates how Proto-AGI is emerging through the integration of specialized AI systems across multiple development tracks. The visualization shows key milestones from major AI organizations between 2020-2026.

The "Fast Take-Off" curve shows the accelerating pace of development. Connections between nodes represent influence relationships. Node size represents significance, with organization indicated by shape and color.

Hover over any node to see detailed information about each milestone.

About this Visualization

This interactive visualization is based on the article "Proto-AGI kommt schneller als erwartet" by Christopher Pollin, exploring the evidence for accelerated AGI development through technical innovations, methodological approaches, multi-agent systems, and practical applications.

The emergence of AGI is depicted not as a singular breakthrough but through the integration of specialized systems across different development tracks, with a notable acceleration ("Fast Take-Off") beginning in late 2023.
